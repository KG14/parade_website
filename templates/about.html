AVHS Advanced Computer Science Projects

The Pleasanton Unified School District continuously pushes the envelope between what is known, and what is possible. Recognizing the need to challenge students beyond their current knowledge base in Computer Science, the capstone course, Advanced Computer Science Projects for the Contemporary World, was created in 2023 to allow students to pursue the innovation of the ever-changing technology in the world we live in today. Dabbling in concepts including (but not limited to) Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, Virtual Reality and applications of Data Science, Amador Valley High School Students prepare themselves for not just college, but for careers in the real world. This unique course allows students to dream about what is possible and to actually make it a reality. Learn more about Amador
